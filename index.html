ABOUT US
 
Consilium ’16 – The NSIT Business Conclave promises to be amongst the best business festival in Delhi with the events being spread over a 3-day period from 12th – 14th February 2016.
With an aim to widen our reach so as to be able to provide a platform for the young, talented, budding economists to test, exhibit and hone their financial knowledge and skills, the Finance and Economics Society of NSIT will be organizing it’s inter-college fest, for the second time, at an even greater magnitude following last year’s success. The Finance and Economics Society of NSIT, fully aware of the engineering background that the college proudly boasts of, believes that interest has no boundaries and the determination to pursue counts the most. We look forward to your participation.

SAMAVESH
The Social Case Study

The Finance and Economics Society of Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology (NSIT) present Samavesh: the Social Case Study Competition. The competition supports the creation of social ventures and projects, which aims to bring about positive social change in a sustainable manner.
The event will consist of an online preliminary round followed by an on campus study round to showcase socially viable projects and their implementation.

THE WHITE COLLAR JOB
The Finance Case Study

Come live the life of an investment banker and test yourself if you have it in you what it takes to become one! To make things a little easier, you have to participate in teams of 2! A case based simulation of the tasks of a banker is the deal of which a few of them are portfolio management, M&A consulting, valuation of a firm. The event consists of an online preliminary round followed by two on- campus rounds.

BEST MANAGER

The Finance and Economics Society of Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology (NSIT) presents to you "Best Manager". Put yourself in the shoes of a leader and decide what is best for your company in the event of a crisis. This is a platform, which tests your managerial and analytical skills, when your company faces pressure. The event will consist of an online preliminary round and an on-campus round.

NSE
The NSIT Stock Exchange

Round 1: Online preliminary round Can you navigate the ups and downs of the stock market? Come test your predictions and leave the rest on the market. NSE brings the real world stock prediction, where the time will be your greatest friend and, fluctuations, your biggest enemy. NITI AYOG
